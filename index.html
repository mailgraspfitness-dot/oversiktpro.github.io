Core product features

A live valuation assistant built for serious used-car buyers.

OversiktPro turns a raw listing into a clearer market view, combining live listing data, comparable signals, tracking context, and practical confidence indicators.

📄

Live listing analysis

Read key signals directly from the FINN listing, including brand, model, year, mileage, seller type, price, and listing identity.

🎯

Deal score with context

Instantly understand whether a car looks overpriced, fairly priced, or unusually attractive relative to estimated market value.

📊

Market value estimation

Surface an estimated market value and potential profit so undervalued listings stand out immediately.

🛡️

Transparent confidence

OversiktPro does not just give you a number. It also shows confidence so users understand when the estimate is strong and when caution is needed.

⏱️

Price history and tracking

See if a listing is new, how long it has been on the market, whether the price was reduced, and how the listing has evolved over time.

✨

Discovery beyond the listing

Track newly discovered deal candidates from a continuously monitored FINN dataset, then verify them with live listing analysis.
